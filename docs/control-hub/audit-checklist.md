# Control Hub Audit Checklist

**Product area:** Control Hub  
**Audience:** Admins, support leads, implementation engineers  
**Last reviewed:** 2026-06-04  
**Source type:** Operational checklist based on official Control Hub concepts

## Purpose

Use this checklist when taking over a customer tenant, preparing for go-live, or reviewing an existing Webex deployment.

## Organization

- Confirm organization name and customer ownership.
- Confirm partner access, if applicable.
- Review organization-level settings.
- Review domains and claimed/verified domains if applicable.

## Administrators

- Review full administrators.
- Review external administrators.
- Review read-only and support administrators.
- Remove stale admin access.
- Confirm admin access follows least privilege.

## Users

- Confirm active users.
- Confirm license assignment.
- Check suspended or inactive users.
- Confirm key users have correct services.

## Locations

- Review location list.
- Confirm addresses and intended use.
- Review numbers assigned to each location.
- Confirm emergency calling requirements are addressed.

## Calling

- Review main numbers.
- Review auto attendants, call queues, hunt groups, and schedules.
- Review voicemail and routing behavior.
- Confirm test calls complete successfully.

## Devices and workspaces

- Review device inventory.
- Confirm device assignments.
- Confirm offline or stale devices.
- Validate a sample physical phone, Webex App, and workspace device if available.

## Contact Center

- Review tenant setup.
- Review users, teams, profiles, queues, entry points, and flows.
- Confirm business hours and routing.
- Validate test inbound and outbound scenarios.

## Evidence to save

- Date reviewed
- Reviewer name
- Customer organization
- Major findings
- Risks
- Recommended remediation
- Official documentation links used
