## Purpose

Document how to add users, assign licenses, confirm activation, and validate access to Webex services.

## Standard process

1. Confirm the organization.
2. Confirm the user's email address.
3. Assign the required licenses.
4. Confirm service entitlements.
5. Send or resend activation if needed.
6. Validate sign-in and product access.

## Common issues

| Symptom | Check |
|---|---|
| User cannot access a service | Confirm license assignment and role. |
| User cannot administer a feature | Confirm admin role and product-specific privilege. |
| Activation not complete | Resend activation and confirm email delivery. |
