# TS: Webex Meetings

Use this page for Webex Meetings issues involving joining, audio, video, recording, scheduling, lobby behavior, host/cohost permissions, and meeting policies.

## First checks

- Confirm whether the issue affects the host, a participant, or everyone.
- Confirm whether the user joined from Webex App, browser, mobile, phone dial-in, or video device.
- Confirm whether the meeting was scheduled from Webex, Outlook, Google Calendar, or an integration.
- Confirm the meeting time, timezone, meeting number/link, and affected user.

## Join issue checklist

| Symptom | Check |
|---|---|
| Cannot join | Meeting link, meeting number, site, user authentication, lobby settings |
| Audio issue | Selected speaker/mic, browser permission, PSTN/audio option, network |
| Video issue | Camera permission, selected camera, bandwidth, background effects |
| Missing recording | Host permissions, recording setting, storage, processing status |
| Calendar issue | Scheduler add-in, calendar integration, invite format |

## Evidence to collect

- Meeting link or meeting number
- Host email
- Affected participant email
- Join method
- Date, time, and timezone
- Screenshot of error
- Browser/app version
- Whether issue happens in another browser/device
