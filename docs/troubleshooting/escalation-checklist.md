# Escalation Checklist

Before escalating, confirm the packet includes enough detail for another engineer or Cisco TAC to investigate without starting over.

## Required

- Clear problem statement
- Business impact
- Affected users/locations
- Exact timestamps with timezone
- Product area
- Configuration screenshots
- Recent changes
- Reproduction steps
- Logs, IDs, or call examples

## Avoid

- Vague statements like "calls are broken"
- Missing timestamps
- Missing user emails
- Missing call examples
- Screenshots without context
