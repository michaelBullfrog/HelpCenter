## Purpose

Use this checklist for initial Webex Contact Center setup and launch preparation.

## Setup phases

1. Confirm subscription and licensing.
2. Complete tenant setup in Control Hub.
3. Configure users, teams, roles, and profiles.
4. Configure voice channel options.
5. Configure entry points, queues, flows, business hours, and routing.
6. Configure desktop profiles and agent experience.
7. Configure reporting and Analyzer access.
8. Test inbound, outbound, transfer, consult, queue, voicemail, and recording scenarios.
9. Document support ownership and escalation paths.
